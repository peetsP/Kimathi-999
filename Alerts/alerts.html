Kimathi 999 - Who are we?

This is a website that is used to inform students and also residents around the institution the security status of the school. Kimathi 999 enables users to be in touch with the local authorities and medical teams.

Students and staffs will be able to receive security information based on their location. Reports abt a malicious activity or suspects can also be posted in this website.

It also has been extended to give additional fuctionality of medical services. Which enable users to get in touch with the school in case of health emergencies.
